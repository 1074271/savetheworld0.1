What is the Greenhouse Effect?

The Greenhouse Effect is an effect that get affect the whole world. With the Greenhouse Effect, Global Warming will be caused, and nobody likes Global Warming. Global Warming is the process of heat growing in the world due to the sun, we have an atmosphere that protects us from the harmful UV rays from the sun. Those UV rays could burn your skin and cause skin cancer.

What is the Ozone Layer?

The Ozone Layer is a layer around the earth that protects the earth from the suns harmful UV rays. The Ozone Layer is made up of 90% ozone gas. Without the ozone layer, earth could potentially be burned into a hot pile of mud and there would be no more source of life on earth anymore.
